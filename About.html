Our Story

Explore Canada was founded in 2020 with a single mission: to share the breathtaking beauty of Canada with the world. From the majestic peaks of the Rockies to the quaint streets of Quebec, we believe in creating unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the diversity and splendor of Canada.

What We Do

Our team curates travel packages, guides, and tips to help travelers of all kinds discover Canada. Whether you're seeking adventure in the great outdoors or a peaceful retreat into nature, Explore Canada is your gateway to the best experiences the country has to offer.

On-Trip Support

Experience unwavering peace of mind with our On-Trip Support, offering 24/7 assistance for any needs or emergencies that may arise during your travels. Whether you encounter a last-minute itinerary change, need local recommendations, or face an unexpected emergency, our dedicated support team is just a call or message away, ensuring you're never alone, no matter where you are in the world. For immediate assistance, simply contact us at +1-800-TRAVEL-HELP. In case of urgent help, send us the signal "SOS-TRAVEL" through our app or messaging service, and our rapid response team will spring into action to provide you with the support you need, ensuring your safety and comfort throughout your journey. With us by your side, you can explore with confidence, knowing that a helping hand is always available, anytime, anywhere.
